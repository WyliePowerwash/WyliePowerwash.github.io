Our Story

At Wylie Wilson Powerwashing, we treat every property like our own. We noticed how many driveways, sidewalks, and homes looked aged and worn out purely from surface dirt, mold, and environmental residue. We invested in top-tier machinery, rotating surface cleaners, and specialized nozzles to solve that problem efficiently.

We balance high-pressure execution for tough concrete stains with delicate, highly accurate soft-washing setups for roofs and vinyl siding. No shortcuts, no damage—just incredibly clean results that instantly boost your property's curb appeal.
